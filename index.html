Inside D

The field hockey fan app — one home for Europe's leagues.

What Inside D does

  • Live scores & matchdays — weekend-grouped fixtures, live ticker, penalty-shootout detail.
  • Standings — always current, with playoff and relegation zones per league.
  • Playoff brackets — championship final rounds and European tournaments, visualised.
  • Push notifications — kickoff, goals and full-time for your favourite teams.
  • Club pages — squads, results and head-to-head across competitions.
  • Bilingual — German and English today; more languages planned.
