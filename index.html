The app

Everything a hockey fan needs

Built for the way fans actually follow the sport: a quick look at the weekend, the table at a glance, and a nudge when your team scores.

  • Live scores

    Weekend-grouped matchdays, a live ticker and full penalty-shootout detail.

  • Standings

    Always current, with playoff and relegation zones marked per league.

  • Playoff brackets

    Championship final rounds and European tournaments, clearly visualised.

  • Push notifications

    Match start, every goal and the final whistle for the teams you follow.

  • Club pages

    Squads, results and head-to-head records across competitions.

  • Two languages

    German and English today, with more on the roadmap.

Coverage

One home for European hockey

Inside D covers national leagues in six countries, from the continent's strongest divisions to growing hockey nations, alongside Europe's premier club tournaments. Men's and women's competitions, senior level.

Currently in closed testing. App store launch in preparation, and the coverage map keeps growing.
